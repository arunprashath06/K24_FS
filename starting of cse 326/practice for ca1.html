consuming a variety of colourful fruits and vegetables ensure you get a wide range of nnutrients.For example:


  • Red FoodsTomatoes,strawberries and watermelon are in high in antioxidants.

Stay Active

Regular physical activites is just as important as balanced diet.Aim for atleast 150 minutes of moderate activity each week. Some great ways to stay active include:

  1. Walking:A simple yet effective way to get moving.
  2. Yoga:Enhances flexibility and mental well-being.
  3. Strength Training:Builds muscles and supports metabolic health.
